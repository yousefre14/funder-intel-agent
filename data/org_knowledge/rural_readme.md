📌 About Us
The Rural Opportunity Institute (ROI) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to strengthening rural communities by addressing the root causes of substance use disorders, adverse childhood experiences (ACEs), and related behavioral health challenges. ROI works at the intersection of public health, community development, and behavioral health to build resilient rural communities.

🎯 Mission
ROI's mission is to build the capacity of rural communities to prevent and address substance use disorders, adverse childhood experiences, and related behavioral health issues through training, technical assistance, and community engagement.

🌍 Vision
A future where all rural communities have the resources, knowledge, and capacity to support the health, well-being, and resilience of every individual and family.

🧭 Who We Are
Rural Opportunity Institute is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization founded to bridge the gap in resources and support available to rural communities. The organization recognizes that rural areas face unique challenges — including geographic isolation, limited access to healthcare, workforce shortages, economic hardship, and stigma — that require tailored, community-driven solutions.

ROI is composed of a team of experts in:

Behavioral health
Community development
Public health
Training and technical assistance
Program evaluation
Rural policy
🔑 Core Focus Areas
1. 🧠 Substance Use Prevention & Recovery
Addressing the opioid crisis and other substance use disorders disproportionately affecting rural America.
Supporting prevention, treatment, and recovery ecosystems in underserved rural regions.
2. 👶 Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACEs)
Raising awareness about the impact of ACEs on lifelong health outcomes.
Building trauma-informed communities and systems.
3. 💪 Community Resilience
Helping rural communities develop the infrastructure and capacity to respond to behavioral health crises.
Promoting protective factors that foster community well-being.
4. 📚 Training & Technical Assistance
Providing evidence-based training to community leaders, organizations, healthcare providers, and coalitions.
Offering technical assistance to help communities implement effective strategies.
5. 🤝 Community Engagement & Coalition Building
Supporting the development of local coalitions and collaborative partnerships.
Empowering communities to lead their own change efforts.

🌱 Our Approach
ROI takes a holistic, community-centered approach that includes:

Listening to Communities — Centering the voices and experiences of rural residents.
Leveraging Local Assets — Building on existing strengths and resources within communities.
Evidence-Based Practices — Utilizing proven strategies and best practices.
Collaboration — Fostering cross-sector partnerships and collective impact.
Sustainability — Helping communities develop long-term capacity for change.
Equity — Ensuring that all community members, including marginalized populations, have access to support and opportunity.
